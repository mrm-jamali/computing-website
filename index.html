About Us

We will express the output speed in two ways. The free speed is the mechanism's output speed when no load is connected. The loaded speed is the output speed when the load force is applied. These speeds can be expressed as either linear velocities, vf ree and vload, or rotational velocities, ωf ree and ωload. In all cases, the speed in question is a steady-state speed, meaning the mechanism has been powered on for enough time to reach it's speed. It does not take into account the acceleration of the mechanism while in start-up or as the load changes

This model provide simple calculations for 4-run method of balancing. To perform balancing with this method, you need a simple vibrometer that can measure the vibration level.
